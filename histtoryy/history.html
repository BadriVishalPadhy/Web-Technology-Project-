HISTORY

The School of Computer Science & Engineering at XIM University, Bhubaneswar, has a rich history and a strong focus on quality education and innovation. Here are some key points about its background: Establishment: It was established as part of Xavier University, which was founded in June 2013 when the Government of Odisha passed the Xavier University Act1. Legacy: The school inherits the legacy of the Xavier Institute of Management, Bhubaneswar, a premier B-School in the country2. Programs Offered: It offers B.Tech. (Hons.) in Computer Science & Engineering, M.Tech in Data Science & Analytics, and Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering3. Focus on Innovation: The school emphasizes sound computer science fundamentals, coding, research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
