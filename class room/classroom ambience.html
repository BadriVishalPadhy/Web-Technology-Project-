Ambience of Classrooms

  • Air-Conditioned Lecture Halls: The lecture halls are equipped with comfortable seating, ensuring a conducive environment for focused learning.

  • Seminar Rooms: These rooms facilitate interactive discussions, workshops, and seminars. They encourage active participation and engagement among students.

  • Multimedia Hall: This space is designed for multimedia presentations, guest lectures, and other audio-visual learning experiences.

  • Computer Labs: Equipped with modern technology, the computer labs allow students to explore practical applications, conduct research, and enhance their digital skills.

  • Executive Classrooms: These specialized rooms offer a professional setting for executive education programs and specialized courses.

  • Educational Audio/Video Equipment: The classrooms feature audio and video systems, enhancing the teaching and learning experience.

  • Tablet PCs and Computers: Students have access to tablet PCs and computers with large monitors, enabling digital note-taking, research, and collaborative projects.
