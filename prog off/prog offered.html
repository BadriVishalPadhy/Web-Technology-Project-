Programs Offered

B.Tech(Hons.) Computer Science & Engineering

The B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering program at XIM University is a four-year, full-time course designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge in the field of computer science. Here are the key details:

  1. Program Overview
    • Duration: 4 years
    • Specialization: CSE(hons)
  2. Eligibility Criteria
    • Candidates must have completed Class XII (or equivalent) in the Science stream (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) with at least 60% marks in aggregate.
    • Final year +2 students expecting their results during 2024 can also apply. Offers of admission to such candidates will be provisional and subject to meeting all degree requirements by July 15, 2024
  3. Program Objectives
    • The B.Tech. CSE program aims to shape students as passionate coders, designers, researchers, and entrepreneurs.
    • Students learn coding languages, develop and analyze algorithms, understand systems, and implement software across various programming languages and platforms.
  4. Fee Waiver
    • Students with 90% marks in aggregate in +2 examination are eligible for a 50% course tuition fee waiver at the time of admission.

M.tech Computer Science & Engineering

The M.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering program offered by the School of Computer Science and Engineering at XIM University is a two-year, full-time postgraduate course. Here are the key details:

  1. Eligibility Criteria(Any one of the below)
    • M.Sc. in Computer Science, Information Technology, Mathematics, Statistics, or related disciplines
    • B.E./B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering, Information Technology, Electrical, Electronics, or related disciplines.
    • MCA with at least 55% marks in aggregate from a recognized university.
  2. Program Highlights
    • The curriculum blends machine learning, programming, and business-oriented subjects.
    • Students engage in an internship and a capstone project, fostering interaction with the data science community and practical application of data science knowledge.
  3. Fee Waiver
    • Students with 90% marks in aggregate in graduation are eligible for a 50% course/tuition fee waiver at the time of admission.
