ETHNIC DAY 2023

The Ethnic Day organized by the Media Cell at the School of Computer Science and Engineering was a resounding success, celebrating the rich tapestry of cultures across India. The event was a vibrant showcase of diversity, bringing together students, faculty, and staff to revel in the colors, flavors, and traditions that make India so unique.

The preparations for the Ethnic Day began well in advance, with enthusiastic participation from students belonging to different regions and cultural backgrounds. The Media Cell played a pivotal role in coordinating the event, ensuring that every aspect was meticulously planned and executed.

One of the highlights of the Ethnic Day was the cultural performances, where students showcased their talents through dance, music, and theatrical presentations. From energetic Bollywood routines to graceful classical performances, the audience was treated to a mesmerizing display of artistic expression.

The success of the Ethnic Day was a testament to the spirit of unity and inclusivity that thrives within the SCSE community. By celebrating India's rich cultural heritage in all its glory, the event fostered a sense of pride and appreciation for diversity, leaving a lasting impact on everyone who participated.
