XAVIER INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE - 2024

The Xavier International Conference on AI (XICAI) held at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, XIM University, was a landmark event that brought together leading experts, researchers, and practitioners in the field of artificial intelligence. Running from February 29th to March 2nd, 2024, the conference provided a platform for the exchange of ideas, insights, and innovations shaping the future of AI.

The inauguration ceremony marked the commencement of the conference, graced by esteemed dignitaries including Father Vice Chancellor, Dr. Santrupt Misra, and Dr. Ganapati Panda. Their presence underscored the significance of the event and highlighted the commitment of SCSE and XIM University to advancing AI research and education.

Throughout the conference there were keynote speeches, panel discussions, paper presentations, and workshops covering AI-related topics. Speakers from reputed Indian and international institutes offered insights into cutting-edge research areas including machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, robotics, and ethical considerations in AI. Attendees had the opportunity to learn from experts, gain insights into emerging trends, and network with professionals and researchers

In addition to the academic and technical sessions, the conference also featured a cultural event that added a vibrant and enriching dimension to the proceedings. Attendees were treated to captivating performances showcasing the rich cultural heritage of India, including traditional dances, music recitals, and artistic displays. The cultural event served as a reminder of the importance of embracing diversity and celebrating the rich tapestry of cultures that make India a truly unique and vibrant nation.

XICAI was a resounding success, fostering collaboration, innovation, and knowledge exchange in the dynamic field of artificial intelligence. By bringing together experts and enthusiasts from around the world, the conference played a crucial role in advancing the frontiers of AI research and shaping the future of technology in a rapidly evolving world.
