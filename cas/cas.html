CAREER ADVISORY SERVICES

What is CAS ?

Career Advisory Services aim to empower individuals to make informed decisions about their careers, navigate the job market effectively, and develop the skills and strategies necessary to achieve their career goals. These services can be particularly beneficial for students, recent graduates, mid-career professionals seeking advancement or career change, and individuals re-entering the workforce after a hiatus.
